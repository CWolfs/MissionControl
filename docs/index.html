Replayability

From new contract types, creative reuse of maps, random spawns and dropping additional lances - every fight feels different!

Unique Custom Content

The only mod that achieves encounter content, such as custom contract types, including new single player contracts, and reuse of single player maps for generic contracts.

Heavily Configurable

Every feature is configurable which allows for a modpack team or player to tweak things to how they like it. Already used in modpacks such as RogueTech and BTA 3062.
