Support

I'm Richard 'CWolf' Griffiths. I'm a developer at a small games company called Rogue Vector who also works on projects on the side. My main side project is modding for BattleTech game and I've created a few useful mods, such as BTDebug, ConverseTek and Mission Control.

If you enjoy my mods and my efforts to bring new content and experiences to BattleTech - feel free to support me with Patreon or by Buying me a Coffee.

Another way to support me is to play with Mission Control and provide feedback, bug reports and ideas. You can find me in the Mission Control discord or any of the main BattleTech game servers (BATTLETECHGAME, RogueTech, BTA3062).
